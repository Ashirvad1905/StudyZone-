About BYJU'S

BYJU'S is an Indian educational technology and online tutoring firm founded by Byju Raveendran. It offers a wide range of educational products and services for students of all age groups, including interactive video lessons, personalized learning programs, and test preparation modules.

Features

  • Engaging video lessons and interactive learning modules.
  • Adaptive learning technology that customizes lessons based on individual student's pace and understanding.
  • Practice quizzes and mock tests to assess learning progress.
  • Live classes and doubt-solving sessions with expert teachers.
  • Comprehensive study materials and e-books.
