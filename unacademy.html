About Unacademy

Unacademy is India's largest online learning platform, empowering millions of learners with knowledge. It offers a comprehensive range of courses taught by the best educators and industry experts, enabling students to learn at their own pace and excel in their academic pursuits.

Features

  • Interactive video lessons and live classes by top educators.
  • Personalized learning paths and adaptive learning techniques.
  • Practice quizzes, mock tests, and study materials for exam preparation.
  • Real-time doubt resolution and discussion forums for enhanced learning.
  • Regular webinars and workshops by industry professionals.

Subjects

Unacademy offers courses in various subjects, including:

  • UPSC and State PSCs
  • Banking and Finance
  • SSC and Railway Exams
  • GATE and ESE
  • CA and CS Exams
  • JEE and NEET
  • English Language and Literature
  • Programming and Technology
