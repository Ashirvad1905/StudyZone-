About Physics Wallah

Physics Wallah is an online platform founded by Alakh Pandey, a renowned physics teacher in India. It provides free educational resources and online coaching for students preparing for various competitive exams, primarily in the field of physics.

Features

  • Comprehensive video lectures covering various topics in physics.
  • Live classes and doubt-solving sessions.
  • Study materials, notes, and practice questions.
  • Mock tests and quizzes for exam preparation.
  • Discussion forums for students to interact and ask questions.

Online Courses

Physics Wallah offers online courses for the following exams:

  • JEE (Joint Entrance Examination) Main and Advanced
  • NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test)
  • AIIMS (All India Institute of Medical Sciences)
  • CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) Physics
